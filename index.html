It starts with a foundation of revolutionary, energy-saving appliances.

Our products are purposefully engineered to make it easier for our customers to lead healthier, happier lives. They begin their life being manufactured in our carbon-neutral factories, using a wide array of recycled materials in their production.

What goes into making a Healthy Kitchen?

What goes into making a Healthy Kitchen?

We work with a select group of like-minded kitchen designers to bring these spaces to life.

Product design and kitchen design go hand in hand, which is why we bring our kitchen designers in at the beginning of the process to help guide our product innovations. Form and function, working together seamlessly.
View Our Council

We partner with some of the worlds foremost nutritionists to understand the best things to prepare in a Healthy Kitchen.

Our partnership with Dole Food Company is one such example. Dole and Beko share twin commitment to sustainable, healthy living and bringing our two brands together will put nutrition, recipe-prep and food preservation at the forefront of American kitchen design.
View Our Partners

You cant have a Healthy Kitchen without a healthy planet.

Beko is firmly committed to sustainability and the long-term health of our planet. But we cant do it alone. It is the bringing together of like-minded individuals and corporations who share our vision to reimagine the American kitchen and beyond that will ultimately lead to a healthier, happier world for us all.

We are all connected to one other.

And together, we are committed

to building a healthier world.

Because you cant have a Healthy Kitchen

without a healthy planet.

At Beko, we are committed to bringing the Healthy Kitchen to life.

By partnering with like-minded individuals and companies,

this vision has become a reality.
